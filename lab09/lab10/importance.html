Why is WebRTC Important?

WebRTC is important because it makes real-time communication more efficient. Most of the time, you have to download apps or plugins to make voice or video calls. But with WebRTC, you don’t need to download anything — you can get straight to communicating with less lag.

It creates faster and more secure connections. WebRTC uses peer-to-peer communication, which means lower latency and higher quality. It’s also encrypted, so your data is more secure during calls and chats.

Nowadays in the digital world, online and remote learning are part of daily life — and WebRTC plays a major role. It runs in the background of many apps we use today for video calls, live chats, and screen sharing, all without needing to download anything.
